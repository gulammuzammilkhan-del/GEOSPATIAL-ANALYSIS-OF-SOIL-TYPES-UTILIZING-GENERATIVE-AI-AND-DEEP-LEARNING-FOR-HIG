Methodology

Three Steps to
Soil Clarity

Our pipeline takes raw field photographs through a battle-tested GAN convolutional network trained on thousands of soil images.

01

Upload Sample

Photograph your soil sample in natural daylight. Our model accepts images in JPG, PNG, or WEBP format and auto-resizes to 150×150px for processing.

02

Neural Analysis

The GAN network processes texture, color gradients, and surface patterns through 16 weight layers to extract soil-specific visual signatures.

03

Crop Recommendation

Based on the classified soil type, the system returns curated vegetable and fruit crop recommendations optimized for that soil's chemical and physical properties.
